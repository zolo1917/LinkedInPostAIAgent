BEAT Cholesterol issues with Oatey!

Did u know? Oat milk & Soya milk help lower your chances of heart diseases, strokes and atherosclerosis (deposit of fat on inner walls of arteries).

OAT MILK consumption: Lowered plasma Cholesterol by 4% & LDL Cholesterol by 9%

In this study the effects of oat milk, soya milk and cow’s milk on plasma lipid, glucose, insulin, and antioxidant status were compared in 24 healthy men and women. Half of the subjects (group A) consumed 0.75–1 litres/day of oat milk & soya milk for 4 weeks each, and the other half (group B) consumed oat milk & cow’s milk for two 4-week periods.

It was found : the high content of βeta-glucans in oat milk was responsible for the decreased plasma cholesterol & LDL cholesterol concentrations.

It is concluded that oat milk can be used as an alternative to other milk drinks by subjects who would benefit from reduced LDL cholesterol values.

Hatti Kaapi and Oatey Come Together to Launch New Range of Vegan Beverages

Bengaluru-based Hatti Kaapi, a homegrown coffee chain with outlets in several Indian cities, has launched a range of vegan filter coffee.

Hatti Kaapi partnered with Oatey, an Indian oatmilk startup, to introduce an oatmilk-based range of vegan filter coffee and masala chai at their highly popular Indiranagar outlet and the newly-launched outlet inside the Bangalore Club.
